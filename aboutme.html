Recent Activities:

Junior Analysis Seminar
This year, Animesh Biswas and myself are establishing a Junior Analysis Seminar at Iowa State. The seminar is for graduate students of all levels interested in analysis.

Joint AWM-MOCA Speaker Series (JAMSS)
I am a co-founder of JAMSS which is run by the Iowa State Association of Women in Mathematics (AWM) Chapter and Mathematicians of Color Alliance (MOCA). I served on and was chair of the organizing committee for the 2017-18 and 2018-19 school years. The series brings four-six underrepresented mathematicians to campus to speak at our departmental colloquium, lead a discussion on his/her experiences as a mathematician, and have one-on-one meetings with faculty and graduate students.

Midwest Mathematics Inclusion and Diversity Workshop for Undergraduates (MMIDWU)
I was part of an organizing committee at Iowa State hosting MMIDWU. The workshop was designed to expose undergraduates to new mathematics as well as to discuss and reflect on the experiences of members of underrepresented groups of mathematicians. There were five featured speakers of various backgrounds and research interests. Each talk was prefaced with a graduate run introductory session.

Mathematics Graduate Student Organization (MathGSO)
As a second year graduate student, I was elected to MathGSO, a group working to address the needs of the graduate students as well as host career day and socials. I spent one year as treasurer and another as the graduate student liaison which included attending the Mathematics Graduate Committee to represent the graduate students.

Association of Women in Mathematics (AWM)
In November 2017, a group of graduate students started an AWM Chapter at Iowa State. I served as interim secretary while the chapter was being formed and spent some time on the events committee.
