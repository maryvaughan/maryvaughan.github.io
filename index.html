Welcome!
My name is Mary Vaughan, and I am currently a RTG Postdoctocal Scholar at the University of Texas at Austin working with Luis Caffarelli, Matías Delgadino, and Stefania Patrizi.

I earned my Ph.D. in Mathematics from Iowa State University in May 2020. My advisor was Pablo Raúl Stinga.

My research is in analysis and partial differential equations. I use techniques from PDEs, harmonic analysis, and functional analysis to study nonlocal and fractional equations. You can learn more on my research page.
The Dreams

In the broad light of day mathematicians check their equations and their proofs leaving no stone unturned in their search for rigor.
But, at night, under the full moon, they dream, they float among the stars and wonder at the miracles of the heavens.
They are inspired. Without dreams there is no art, no mathematics, no life.
