Team Based Learning
For the 2018-19 school year, I was funded though the Howard Hughes Medical Institute grant. In collaboration with Anna Aboud and under the direction of Heather Bolles, we developed Team-Based Learning material for non-TBL instructors to implement active learning in non-TBL classes.

Make It Stick
In 2017, I participated in a Graduate Teaching and Learning Community to read and discuss the book Make It Stick - The Science of Successful Learning by Peter C. Brown. In 2019, I co-led a teaching seminar which included discussions on the book.
